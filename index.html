Soccer Strength & Shoulder Health

Shoulder note: given the bilateral Bankart repair history and two post-op right-shoulder re-dislocations, this program keeps shoulder work in rows, external rotation, scap, and landmine-angle pressing rather than loaded end-range overhead/abduction+external-rotation positions. This isn't medical advice — run the exercise list by your surgeon or PT before starting, and stop anything that reproduces an apprehension or instability feeling.
