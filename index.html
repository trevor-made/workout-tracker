Soccer Strength & Shoulder Health

3x/week · 24 Hour Fitness · 4-week block · Trevor + Blake · one simple full-body session, same lifts every time
ExplosivenessJumps for soccer-specific power and change of direction.
Upper Body StrengthBalanced press/pull volume to build real hypertrophy, not just prehab.
Shoulder DurabilityScap + rotator cuff work every session, kept out of provocative overhead ranges.
Beginner-FriendlySame 11 moves every session, grouped in 5 easy-to-remember pairs/trios. Add weight as it gets easy.
Shoulder note: given the bilateral Bankart repair history and two post-op right-shoulder re-dislocations, this program keeps shoulder work in rows, external rotation, and scap work rather than loaded end-range overhead/abduction+external-rotation positions. This isn't medical advice — run the exercise list by your surgeon or PT before starting, and stop anything that reproduces an apprehension or instability feeling.
